<h2>Software Development</h2>
<p>We support your company's full software development life-cycle, from concept and
requirement and specification phase, offering consulting services on best-practices and
proper use of design-patterns, high-end performance review and optimization, to unit-testing
and quality control. We can ease the development of mission-critical software modules
and integrating these to your current software systems.</p>

<h2>Code Review</h2>
<p>If your company has any difficulty with internally developing software or
with a third-party developer, we can provide a code-review service.
We can review your source code and produce a detailed report on what it does,
how it does that and what are its strong and weak points, and try to predict
future problems.</p>

<h2>Improving Software Performance</h2>
<p>If you are suffering with scalability and performance problems, we can help with a
complete analysis of your software stack to weed out bottlenecks. With monitoring solutions
for production environments to code review to figure out how to squeeze out more performance.</p>

<h2>Software Engineering and Methodologies</h2>
<p>If your company doesn't have a formal software development process, and this is causing
problems, we can help you to implement a simple and effective methodology with little
bureaucracy to help improve your software quality in a few months afters it is implemented.</p>

<h2>API and Systems Integration</h2>
<p>If you need to integrate your system to third party systems, we can help, regardless of the
method - being an API or proprietary protocols or file formats - we can help with this integration.
</p>
